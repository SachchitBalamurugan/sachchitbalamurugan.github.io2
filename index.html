What is a Gavel Club

A Gavel Club is part of the Toastmasters International program tailored for high school students to enhance their public speaking and leadership skills. Through structured activities and materials, members engage in delivering speeches, participating in debates, and receiving constructive feedback in a supportive environment. This helps them build confidence and competence in communication and leadership.
